CIS215 · Selection decision
Choose the coordinate system

What do you know: the name or the position?

Know labels.loc[rows, columns]

“Rows 0-4, City and Population”

Know positions.iloc[row_pos, col_pos]

“First five rows, first two columns”

Rule: Prefer labels when they state your intent.

CIS215 · Combine conditions
Both tests must pass

Parentheses protect each condition.

# Western cities above 1.5 million
west_large = df_cities[
    (df_cities['Region'] == 'West')
    & (df_cities['Population'] > 1_500_000)
]
Los AngelesPhoenix

& means AND. | means OR. Wrap every condition.
