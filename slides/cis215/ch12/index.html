CIS215 · Ethics check
Choose the least risky evidence

A team wants names and ZIP codes for a neighborhood service report.

The report needs service patterns by neighborhood, not contact with individuals. What should the analyst keep, and what must the project record?

Keep the minimum fields and aggregate the result. Remove names. Confirm the ZIP code is needed, document consent and safeguards, and disclose any reporting gaps or proxy risk.
